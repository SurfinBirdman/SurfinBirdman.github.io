This is our story...


It all began February 16th(ish) 2012. An unlikely group was dumped into the middle of doomsday scenario and forced to team up to overthrow an international spy ring. It was during this action-packed adventure that our two protagonaists met.


After their first encounter, the pair (using the same spy skills they exhibited on their first encounter) managed to make contact again and set up a rendevous the following weekend. Despite the maze-like streets of Waltham, they were able to make their way to the Museum of Industry, where they were pleasantly suprised to disvocer how much they had had in common.


Following hours spent perusing the museum like enginologists (a cross between engineer & archaeologists), and a brief dinner, they made their way to a nearby college to watch the amazing fire juggling feats of Torie's fire-dancing troupe.


It was all too soon, but eventually Torie had to go. She had made plans, before the two of them had met, to travel to another collegefor the summer and would be leaving soon. They vowed to keep in touch but agreed they should take a break. Fortunately for this story their break lasted about as long as it takes to fly from New York to Salt Lake City.


Throughout their long distance correspondance, they were planning their reunion. Jared finished school, and flew out to Utah to meet her. From there they took a romantic train-ride through the Sierra-Nevadas and through on to Sacramento.
